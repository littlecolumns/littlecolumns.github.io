Summary

An analysis from ProPublica on the relationship between poverty and performance in Advanced Placement classes. The original data is no longer available, but in comparison to the other, smaller-scale educational analyses (Tampa Bay Times and Dallas Morning News, for example) this shows the wider range possible for national-level data.
